# Drugs

## Description
This dataset is about substance abuse, or usage disorders (cigarettes, marijuana, cocaine, alcohol) among different age groups and states. It includes both total counts and rates per 1000 people. The data ranges from 2002 to 2018. 

## Files
- drugs.csv:  dataset file 

## Source
Data was collected from individual states as part of the NSDUH study (National Survey on Drug Use and Health) conducted by SAMHSA, which is the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration. 

## Notes
- Totals are in thousands of people. Rates are per 1,000 people
- Age groups are covered and split by: 12-17, 18-25, and 26+
- Data is broken down by state
- Source: https://pdas.samhsa.gov/#/
